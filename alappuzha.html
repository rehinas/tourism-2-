About us

welcome to alappuzha

Between the palm-fringed waterways and soothing beaches, don’t you think Alleppey could be your best vacation destination of the year? Known as the ‘Venice of the East’ Alleppey is a tourist destination where nature’s green is gold, its beauty will blaze bright to soothe your eyes and delight your soul. Calm backwaters dotted with thousands of houseboats, lip- smacking Seafood, lovely Ayurvedic spas and wellness centres – Don’t you think Alleppey could be the best getaways in Kerala for a peaceful and amazing vacation? Quaint villages, paddy fields and tranquil backwaters are the richest bounties of Alappuzha. Thousands of tourists from all over the world come to Alleppey to enjoy the beauty of this tropical paradise. This Venetian capital of Kerala is the first choice of Honeymooners as the scenic charm, coconut groves, azure lagoons and amazing backwaters have a dream-like quality to spend some moments of togetherness. It is one of the most amazing tourist destinations to visit in Kerala.

Alappuzha Beach

Alappuzha Beach is a beach in Alappuzha town and a tourist attraction in Kerala, India. Beach has an old pier which extend to sea is over 150 years old. Alappuzha beach host many events annually like Alappuzha beach festival, Sand art festival and many more.

Alleppey Lighthouse

The Alappuzha Lighthouse is situated in the coastal town of Alappuzha, Kerala. It was built in 1862 and is a major tourist attraction. Visitors are allowed between 1500 hours and 1630 hours on every weekday at an admission fee of 20 rupees for Indian citizens and 50 rupees for foreigners
