Popular Destination

The museum of Mattancherry

Popularly known as the Dutch Palace, the Mattancherry Palace is a Portuguese palace in Mattancherry, Kochi, in the state of Kerala. Mattancherry Palace is now a museum displaying paraphernalia belonging to the rajas of Kochi.

Vypin

Known for its long sand beaches, laid-back Vypin Island is a common stop on local ferry tours. Popular Cherai Beach is renowned for dolphin sightings, while quieter Kuzhuppilly Beach is flanked by coconut groves and offers safe swimming
